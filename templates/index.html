Camera Permission

We need access to your camera to detect hand gestures. Please click the button below and then choose "Allow" when your browser prompts you.

Allow camera on mobile browser:

  1. Go to browser settings.
  2. Open Site settings.
  3. Tap Camera.
  4. Set it to Sites can ask.
  5. Find this site and set camera to Allow.
  6. Return here and tap Grant Camera Access again.
